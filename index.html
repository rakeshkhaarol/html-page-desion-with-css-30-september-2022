Google has announced that it is shutting down the cloud-based gaming service ‘Google Stadia’ after nearly three years of its launch. “We’ve made the difficult decision to begin winding down our Stadia streaming service,” Google wrote in a blog post informing about the wrap up.

Google said players will continue to have access to its game library and will play until January 18, 2023, before they can complete their final play session.
